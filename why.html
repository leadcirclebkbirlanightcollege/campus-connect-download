Why Campus Connect Exists

A founder’s perspective on the problem that inspired Campus Connect.

Campus Connect was conceived from a recurring challenge observed within the college ecosystem—fragmented communication. Academic notices, event updates, and official announcements were often distributed across multiple informal platforms, resulting in confusion, missed updates, and delayed information for students.

As a student, I experienced this issue firsthand. Important updates were frequently circulated through unofficial channels, making it difficult to verify authenticity and relevance. This lack of a centralized system highlighted the need for a reliable and institution-aligned communication platform.

Campus Connect was designed to address this gap by introducing a single, verified digital space for campus communication. The objective was not merely to develop an application, but to establish a structured and dependable system that ensures clarity, accountability, and timely access to information.

Today, Campus Connect represents a step towards a more transparent, connected, and student-centric campus experience—where information is accessible, authentic, and efficiently delivered to every stakeholder.

Atharv Jadhav Founder & Project Lead, Campus Connect
