Founder’s Perspective

Why Campus Connect Exists

Not just an app — a response to a real problem faced by students every day.

The Problem

Campus communication had become fragmented. Academic notices, event updates, and official announcements were scattered across WhatsApp groups, notice boards, and unofficial channels — leading to confusion, delays, and missed information.

A Student’s Experience

As a student, I experienced this gap firsthand. Important updates were often received late or lost among informal messages. There was no single, reliable source students could trust for verified campus information.

The Vision

Campus Connect was designed to introduce a centralized, verified digital platform for campus communication. The goal was clarity, accountability, and timely access to information — not noise.

What It Represents

Today, Campus Connect stands for a more transparent, connected, and student-centric campus experience — where information is authentic, accessible, and efficiently delivered.

Atharv Jadhav Founder & Project Lead

“Campus Connect was built to solve a problem I personally faced — and to create a system students can genuinely rely on.”
