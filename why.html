Why Campus Connect Exists

A real campus problem, addressed through a student-led digital initiative.

The Problem

In our college, multiple two-credit certificate courses are conducted every academic term. Attendance for these courses is recorded manually on hard-copy sheets and later transferred to Excel files. This process is time-consuming, error-prone, and difficult to manage at scale.

The Idea

Repeated exposure to this workflow highlighted the need for a centralized digital system. The idea behind Campus Connect was to eliminate fragmented processes and introduce a single, reliable platform for academic coordination and communication.

The Build

Campus Connect was developed with a focus on simplicity, clarity, and institutional alignment. The platform aims to reduce manual effort while maintaining transparency and accuracy for students and coordinators alike.

The Launch

What began as a solution to attendance management evolved into a broader initiative to improve official campus communication. Campus Connect represents a step towards a more connected and efficient academic environment.

Founder’s Note

Campus Connect was initiated after closely observing the operational challenges faced during the management of certificate courses and academic coordination. This platform reflects a sincere effort to simplify processes while maintaining transparency and institutional alignment.

— Atharv Jadhav
Founder & Project Lead, Campus Connect
