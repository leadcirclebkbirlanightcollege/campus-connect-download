Why Campus Connect Exists

A first-hand perspective from the founder on the problem that inspired Campus Connect.

Campus Connect was conceived from a simple but persistent problem observed within our college ecosystem—fragmented communication. Important academic notices, event updates, and official announcements were often scattered across multiple platforms, leading to confusion, delays, and missed information for students.

As a student, I experienced this challenge firsthand. Critical updates were frequently shared through unofficial channels, making it difficult to distinguish verified information from informal circulation. This lack of structure directly affected clarity and accountability.

Campus Connect was designed to address this gap by creating a centralized, verified, and reliable digital platform for campus communication. The goal was not just to build an application, but to introduce a system that institutions and students can trust.

Campus Connect represents a step towards a more transparent, connected, and student-centric campus experience—where information is authentic, accessible, and efficiently delivered.

Atharv Jadhav Founder & Project Lead, Campus Connect
