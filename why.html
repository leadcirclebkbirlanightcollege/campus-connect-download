Why Campus Connect Exists

A first-hand account of the problem that led to the creation of Campus Connect.

Campus Connect emerged from a recurring issue observed within the college ecosystem— fragmented communication. Academic notices, event updates, and official announcements were often scattered across multiple platforms, resulting in confusion and missed information for students.

As a student, I personally experienced this gap. Important updates were frequently circulated through unofficial channels, making it difficult to verify authenticity and relevance. This lack of a centralized system highlighted the need for a dependable and institution-aligned solution.

Campus Connect was therefore designed to introduce a single, verified digital platform that ensures clarity, accountability, and timely access to information. The objective was not merely to build an application, but to establish a structured communication system students can trust.

Today, Campus Connect represents a step toward a more transparent, connected, and student-centric campus experience—where information is accessible, authentic, and efficiently delivered.

Atharv Jadhav Founder & Project Lead, Campus Connect
