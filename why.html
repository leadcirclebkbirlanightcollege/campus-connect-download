Founder’s Note

Why Campus Connect Exists

A real academic problem, approached with a structured digital solution.

The Problem

In our college, multiple two-credit certificate courses are conducted each academic term. Attendance for these courses is recorded on hard-copy sheets and later manually entered into Excel files.

This process is time-consuming, error-prone, and requires repeated coordination, making scalability and accuracy difficult as participation increases.

The Idea

The lack of a centralized digital system highlighted the need for a reliable platform that could streamline attendance and academic coordination.

The Build

Campus Connect was developed as a student-driven initiative focused on clarity, accuracy, and institutional alignment.

The Launch

What began as a solution to attendance management evolved into a broader platform aimed at improving campus communication and transparency.

Atharv Jadhav Founder & Project Lead, Campus Connect
